# Insight Tutors — writing

User-facing copy for Insight Tutors (site content, tutor bios, meta descriptions, form labels, etc.):

- **Never use em dashes or double hyphens as punctuation** (`—`, `--`). Use commas, full stops, colons, or restructure the sentence instead.
- En dashes in number ranges are fine (e.g. `Years 1–10`, `$75–$95`).
